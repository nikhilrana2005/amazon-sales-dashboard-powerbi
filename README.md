## 📌 Project Overview

The **Amazon Sales Dashboard** is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project developed using **Microsoft Power BI**. It analyzes Amazon sales data from **2019–2024** to provide meaningful insights into sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, regional trends, and executive-level decision-making.

This dashboard demonstrates how raw business data can be transformed into interactive visualizations and actionable insights to support strategic business decisions.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Monitor business profitability
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Compare regional sales performance
- Evaluate salesperson performance
- Generate executive business insights
- Support data-driven decision-making
